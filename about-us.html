Vision, Mission & Zionars Band

Vision

Our vision is to Step up the Worship Standard in places of worship so that the Body of Christ can grow a deeper and stronger relationship with God.

Mission

We aspire to offer exposure and training to young talents and teams to step up the standards of praise and worship in respective stations of assignments. Our desire is to see people experiencing deeper worship moments enhanced by musical excellence.

Zionars Band

Zioners Band is a model band whose purpose and assignment is to empower individuals and teams to step up their standards of service through musical excellence. Pastor Denis - Ministerial Highlights

Pastor Dennis brings over 20 years of ministry experience to Mwea Peoples Church (MPC), having served under the leadership of Apostle Dr. Francis Wanderi at The Peoples Church and Rev. Samuel Chomba at Kagumo Peoples Church.

Recognized as Worship Pastor of the Year 2022 by Miruri Awards, he is known for his passionate leadership in creating inspiring worship experiences and connecting with diverse audiences.

Currently leading the Zioners Band and MPC, his skills extend to worship leadership, team coordination, and musician's mentorship. Other engagements include TV & Radio programmes; The perspective on Elevate TV Kuriukia Mwihoko Show on Gichichio TV K24 Rurumuka show - Inooro TV Kumatha Ngai - Kameme TV KTN Uvooro TV Radio Maisha
